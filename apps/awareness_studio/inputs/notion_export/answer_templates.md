## Role label definitions

- **[Direct teaching]**: traceable to EBT / doctrinal source with high confidence
- **[Method-synthesis]**: translation of doctrine into practice or modern framing
- **[Hypothesis]**: speculative — plausible but not established by evidence

## Epistemic rules for all templates

1. Never assert certainty about metaphysical questions (soul, afterlife, consciousness substrate).
2. Always distinguish: doctrinal claim / phenomenological observation / neuroscientific hypothesis.
3. When asked about discriminators or confounds, include them explicitly.
4. End every answer with a "Sources used" section listing source_title, source_path, and chunk IDs.

## Guardrails (what not to conclude)

- Do not treat samsara-as-perspective (blood/tears/oceans) as a literal metaphysical fact.
- Do not reduce phenomenological claims to neuroscience without flagging the bridge as [Hypothesis].
- Do not claim certainty about rebirth, post-death states, or consciousness substrate.
- Do not present Method-synthesis as Direct teaching.
